From Sydney

CountryLink offers three XPLORER rail services daily from Sydney to Canberra. You can catch the XPL from Central Station in Sydney stopping at Strathfield, Campbelltown and seven other stops before Queanbeyan and Canberra. The trip is just over 4 hours.

From Melbourne

There is no direct rail service from Melbourne however Countrylink provides an XPT service that travels daily from Melbourne. Catch a train to Cootamundra, and then get a connecting bus transfer to Canberra.

Alternatively catch the Sydney train from Melbourne and transfer to Canberra from Goulburn railway station.

This option will take just under 10 hours so best you bring a GTM mixtape!

PLANE

Eftpos / ATMs

Cash. You should bring it!

ATMs will be onsite at GTM, however these are always in high demand and can be slow. Best bet is to get organised BEFORE you get to GTM and bring enough cash to get you through the day. Nobody wants to stand in a line when you could be watching your favourite band.

The headspace helpdesk is back again this year to help answer any questions you have about GTM or getting home. Look for the bright green and head over and talk to the friendly team.

Lost & Found

The headspace helpdesk is the place to go to hand in a lost item youve found at the event or to see if someone has handed in an item that you or a friend has lost at the event.

Items not collected at the end of the night:Will be retained at the event site by GTM for two (2) days post event. Stay tuned for collection info.

Cloakroom

GTM will be running a Cloakroom for your convenience, check out the details below;

Cost: One (1) standard cloak is $5.00

What is one (1) standard cloak?

  • 1 article of clothing per hanger
  • Loose items must be able to fit into the equivalent of 1 x A4 Bag
  • 1 x personal bag no larger than a standard day pack

Anyone wishing to cloak items larger than one (1) standard cloak must purchase a second cloak.

Valuables (including mobile phones, wallets, cameras and electrical equipment) will be cloaked at the risk of the customer and must be listed on the cloak tag at the time of cloaking the item. GTM will not take responsibility for items not declared at the time of cloaking.

Customers are responsible for ensuring they retain their wristband / cloak tag throughout the day. Lost wristbands / cloak tags will be asked to wait until the end of the evening to collect items

Accessing your stuff throughout the day:You can access your stuff throughout the day for the cost of $2.00 by way of presenting your wristband / cloak tag.

Cloaked items not collected at the end of the night:Will be retained at the event site by GTM for two (2) days post event. Stay tuned for collection info.
